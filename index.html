TejasKalp is an AI‑assisted drone‑based geospatial reconstruction platform in the research phase, designed to deliver real‑time 3D terrain intelligence directly in the field. Equipped with a daytime aerial imaging drone and an onboard GPU‑enabled edge computing module integrated into the RC/controller system, it captures high‑resolution imagery and generates georeferenced 3D outputs without relying on cloud infrastructure. By supporting GeoJSON‑based workflows, TejasKalp enables rapid structural and terrain reconstruction at significantly lower cost than traditional LiDAR solutions, with applications ranging from city planning and infrastructure mapping to surveillance, situational awareness, and energy or telecom asset monitoring.

Real‑Time 3D Reconstruction

Generates georeferenced terrain and structural models instantly in the field.

Edge‑Powered Processing

Onboard GPU module enables rapid analysis without cloud dependency.

Cost‑Effective Mapping

Provides high‑resolution outputs at a fraction of LiDAR’s operational cost.

Versatile Applications

Supports city planning, surveillance, and energy/telecom asset monitoring.
